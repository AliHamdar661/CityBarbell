“You are what you eat”, “No pain, no gain”, “Stay Hungry”, “Winners do not quit” … All of these are now quotes which people are familiar with, all of them converge towards the same goal: LIFESTYLE! And since a healthy lifestyle backed up by a consistent effort is the clear way to success and having a great shape and attractive body, this is our main focus at “City Barbell” which was established in 2018, hosting now more than 200 members.

What we care about here at “City Barbell” is your lifestyle, the way you live, workout, spend your time, eat and all of what’s related to usual daily habits that will make the best version of you. And since lifestyle is our main aim, our priority was always to keep our gym with an environment that helps you maintain that lifestyle you need in order to succeed. Everything here is designed to make you feel comfortable, enthusiastic, well-equipped and for sure, supported!



You can enjoy our weight lifting area to increase your muscles’ strength, use the functional area to improve your agility and of course, make use of our cardio machines to help upsurge your muscles endurance and lose some non-needed fats. Not forgetting to mention our variety of “Les Mills” classes which will provide you with an unpreceded experience where you are enjoying your workout while getting the most of it with our certified trainers and coaches!
Come and join us! Above all, you will be surprised with the amount of positive vibes our place gives, the ambiance was specifically designed to make you feel excited and comfortable at the same time so you can invest all of your focus on your workout while enjoying it!
